About Path Center Cafe

This Cafe helps support Otino Waa Childrens Village, located in Kole. We employ graduate students ad sell products made by Otino Waa house Moms and Craft artists from around Uganda.

Path Center Cafe offers both Gift Shop items and food products from bakery to intercontinental dishes that suites all culture

All our services offered is to raise funds to support the Orphans to see that they have a bright future and become Uganda's future leaders.
Thank you for choosing PATH Center Cafe and we appreciate your support!
